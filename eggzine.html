5 Eggcellent Ways to Cook an Egg
A zine dedicated to the fundamental and basic ways to cooking an egg. I exercised my skills of word placement and typography through five illustrative ways of cooking an egg.

Objective
Conceive and create a project that utilizes the skills, techniques, and theories discussed and learned throughout the course.
Ideation and Goals

01 â€” Focus on word placement and typography.

Although I did note the specific steps in how to cook the egg, I mainly wanted to incorporate the process of cooking the egg through illustration. Viewers themselves would get a sense on how to cook the egg mainly based on the way the illustrations were laid out.

02 â€” Have the zine look interactive.

To make the zine more interactive and to further instruct those who come across my zine, I had typography incorporated into the illustrations to guide the viewer's eye in the main action of cooking an egg. For example, in order to make a scrambled egg, you need to whisk an egg. In the illustration, the word 'SCAMBLED' is warped in a swirl to indicate the whisking gesture in the process of making scrambled eggs.

03 â€” Inform and instruct the viewer.

I wanted to allow viewers to imagine themselves as if they were cooking the egg by having mostly aerial views incorporation into the illustrations. Viewers themselves would look at the hands and imagine themselves participating into the fun cooking activity. In addition, minutes of how long the egg would take to cook are noted on the bottom right corner of every second page to further inform and instruct the viewer on the amount of cooking time.
