Overview

As a Graphic Artist for The Daily Californian, I aided in creating compelling visual content that accompanied and helped synthesize news and stories.

I designed and revised pieces once every week to produce printed and digital graphics displaying statistics and detailed info to readers in a variety of styles.

Learnings and Takeaways

Throughout my time with the editorial team, I learned how to work in a fast-paced environment and how to communicate effectively. I also earned how to take in and utilize feedback on the graphics I was producing and was able to grow my design and layout skills, especially for printed material.
