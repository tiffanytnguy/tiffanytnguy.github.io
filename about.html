Hello, I’m Tiffany — a Product Designer, based from the Bay Area.
Intrigued by the intersection of design, technology, and educational spaces, I’m interested in designing accessible and inclusive experiences that shape how we interact with digital tools and each other.

I'm currently earning my UX Design Techdegree at Treehouse and I'm a UX Design apprentice at Niantic.


Background
Previously, I studied Art History and Media at the University of California, Berkeley where I focused on the power and complexity of visual spaces. During my time at Cal, I worked as a graphic designer, illustrator, and design lead with a variety of student organizations that helped me emerge and hone my skills as a digital creator, a team spirit animal, and a helpful mentor.

In regards to my experience, I’ve worked with early-stage startups as the sole designer, with company values on self-improvement and fair compensation among underrepresented groups.


Indulgences
Aside from design, I enjoy watching the intricacy of the Gourmet Makes series from Bon Appetit and the wholesome space of BBC’s the Great British Baking Show. Other than that, I like to engross myself in cat videos, puns, and inspirational talks.
