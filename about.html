Background
Previously, I studied Art History and Media at the University of California, Berkeley where I focused on the power and complexity of visual spaces, and learned how to decode our visual world. During my time at Cal, I worked as a graphic designer, illustrator, and design lead with a variety of student organizations that helped me jumpstart and hone my skills as a digital creator, a team spirit animal, and a helpful mentor.

Most of my professional experience resides in crafting digital graphics and UX design, but I’ve explored alternative methods for UX research and VR set design. I’ve worked with early-stage startups as the product designer and graphic designer, with company values on self-improvement and fair compensation among underrepresented groups. Working with both remote and non-remote teams have shaped how I collaborate with teammates and think when solving problems that serve particular communities.
