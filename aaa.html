Background
Asian American Association seeks to develop proactive and community-oriented leaders through a social, educational awareness, and service network. It enhances the quality of life at Berkeley through its focus on diversity appreciation.

As a graphic designer and design lead, I aided in providing the art direction as well as promoting a team community to further emphasize the organization's mission and values.
Objective
Create the marketing graphics for Asian American Association's First General Meeting.
Ideation
"Home Away from Home"
Initially, I created four different design bundles for the board to choose from and this was the one where they felt would most likely entice future members for the club as well as captivating the mission of the club - to provide an Asian American space on campus as well as educating others on Asian American identity. The idea - ‚ÄúHome Away From Home‚Äù - was what I felt would be geared towards incoming freshmen and transfers.
